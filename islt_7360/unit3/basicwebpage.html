It's time to start thinking about project topics for our final websites! Some topic choices I'm considering are:

Topic 1

Happy Tails

I love to take my pup to area dog parks, dog swims, and other pet-friendly venues!
However, finding such places isn't alwasy easy.
I'd like to create a website that allows users to find dog-freindly events, parks, stores (and more) within the Saint Louis, Missouri area.

I was thinking about including such things as:

  • Upcoming Events such as the APA's Canine Carnival
  • Area Dog Parks, such as Dusable Dog Park
  • Pet-Centered Shops, such as Treats Unleashed
  • And More!

Topic 2

The Sweet Spot

I'm also an avid baker! My sister and I love to get together and bake. Our particular favorite is cookies. I was considering a website with
recipes for different cookies and such. I'd like to have them divided up, with different pages for chocolate lovers, cinnamon addicts and the like.
Marian Keyes says it best:

Baking makes me focus. On weighing the sugar. On sieving the flour. I find it calming and rewarding because, in fairness,
it is sort of magic - you start off with all this disparate stuff, such as butter and eggs, and what you end up with is so totally different.
And also delicious.
